How it works

Get on the water

No experience needed. UC Rowing is open to all UC students — whether you've never touched an oar or rowed at nationals.

1
Fill in the form
Complete the membership form on this page with your details. Takes about two minutes.
2
Join our Facebook group
Join the UCRC Facebook group to stay up to date with sessions, events, and club news.
3
Come to a session or event
Regular training runs during Term 1. We also have events and socials throughout the rest of the year — keep an eye on the Facebook group for what's on.
Questions? Email us at president.ucrc@gmail.com
