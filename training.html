Training locations

Where we train

Waimakariri River
McLeans Island Rd, Christchurch
Our primary on-water venue. The boat shed is located on the true left bank. Parking available on site. Sessions depart from the shed — be there 10 minutes before session start.
Lake Rua Roto
Ruapuna, Christchurch
Used for longer rows and race preparation ahead of regattas. Flat water and good wind shelter make it ideal for race-pace work. Transport organised through the club.
UC Gym
Ilam Campus, University of Canterbury
Ergometer sessions are held in the UC Sport facility on campus. Erg machines are booked for club use on Tuesday and Thursday mornings.
Boat Shed
Waimakariri River, McLeans Island
Home base for the club. Equipment storage, changing rooms, and rigging area. Members receive a key code on joining. Please leave the shed clean and secure after use.
